# StremFusion v1.2.0 - Quick Start Guide

Welcome to StremFusion! This guide will help you set up and start mirroring your devices in minutes.

## 📦 What's Included
1. **StremFusion-v1.2.0-Portable.exe**: Run immediately without installation.
2. **StremFusion Setup 1.2.0.exe**: Standard Windows installer for a permanent setup.

---

## 📱 Android Setup (USB)
1. **Enable Developer Options**: Go to Settings > About Phone > Tap 'Build Number' 7 times.
2. **Enable USB Debugging**: Go to Settings > Developer Options > Turn on 'USB Debugging'.
3. **Connect**: Plug your phone into your PC via USB.
4. **Authorize**: Accept the "Allow USB Debugging?" prompt on your phone screen.
5. **Launch**: Open StremFusion and click 'Initialize Scan'.

---

## 📶 Android Setup (Wireless)
1. First, connect your phone via USB and ensure it is detected.
2. In StremFusion, go to the **Wireless ADB** tab.
3. Click **Enable Wireless Mode**.
4. Disconnect the USB cable.
5. Ensure your PC and Phone are on the same WiFi network.
6. Enter your Phone's IP address (found in WiFi settings) and click **Establish Wireless Link**.

---

## 🍎 iOS Setup
1. Connect your iPhone/iPad via an original Apple cable.
2. Tap **"Trust this Computer"** and enter your passcode on the device.
3. In StremFusion, go to the **iOS Mirror** tab and click **Initialize iOS Scan**.
4. Note: File system access requires iTunes or Apple Devices drivers to be installed on Windows.

---

## 📂 Asset Explorer (Smart View)
- Click the **Files** icon to browse your device files.
- **Open (Eye Icon)**: View photos/videos directly on your PC.
- **Push**: Drag and drop files from your PC into the Asset Explorer window to upload them.

---

## 🛠 Troubleshooting
- **Device not found?** Try another USB port or cable. Ensure debugging is ON.
- **Laggy Mirror?** Use a USB 3.0 port and 5GHz WiFi for wireless mode.
- **Black Screen?** Ensure your phone screen is unlocked.
